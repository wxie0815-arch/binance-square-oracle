# Crypto Content Writing Rules

Use these rules when turning market data into a Binance Square article.

## Core Rules

- Lead with the strongest signal first.
- Keep claims tied to visible data.
- Distinguish fact from interpretation.
- Prefer concrete numbers over vague adjectives.
- Avoid obvious AI filler, empty hype, and repeated sentence patterns.
- Do not promise profit or certainty.
- Mention risk conditions when the setup is fragile.

## Binance Square Fit

- Optimize for short, sharp, mobile-friendly reading.
- Keep paragraphs compact.
- End with a useful takeaway, not a generic disclaimer.
- Use hashtags sparingly and only when relevant.
- Mention coins only when they actually appear in the analysis.

## Tone

- Confident but not reckless.
- Insightful, direct, and publish-ready.
- No "as an AI" language.
- No fake access claims.

## Suggested Structure

1. Hook
2. Key market signal
3. Supporting evidence
4. Risk or invalidation
5. Takeaway for Binance Square readers
